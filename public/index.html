About The Pot Zone

In the beginning..

In August 2014, we opened as the first recreational marijuana store in Kitsap County. Starting with only three strains behind the counter, we have come a long way! Today we pride ourselves on our variety! We offer many products such as bud, edibles, concentrates etc. With a wide range of THC and CBD levels, we remain true to who we are and continuously strive to present you the best products available.

When you enter our store you will be greeted by a very knowledgeable, personable staff. We enjoy keeping good relationships with our customers. We love having our customers leave with the product they desired and a smile on their face!

With daily sales, quality product and impeccable service Pot Zone is the right choice for you!

Whether you have consumed products for years or you’re just starting out, come on down today!
